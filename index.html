The planned experience

A considered recommendation in three steps.

The quiz and matching system will be added in later development stages. This first version establishes the experience around them.

  1. 01

    Share your priorities

    Answer plain-language questions about how, where and why you use a laptop.

  2. 02

    Compare the fit

    Your answers will be assessed against a small, carefully structured dataset.

  3. 03

    Understand the result

    See a focused recommendation with clear reasons and acknowledged trade-offs.
